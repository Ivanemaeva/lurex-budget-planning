# LUREX — Commercial & Production Budget Planning

Bachelor's degree project (Economia Aziendale) — Group "Ctrl+Alt+Budget".

## Overview

Full-year budget planning model built for LUREX, a fictional/case-study footwear manufacturer (women's shoes) founded in the 1960s in Castelfranco di Sotto, Italy. The project builds a commercial budget and a production budget from scratch, following standard management-control methodology.

## Content

- **Commercial Budget**: sales forecasting and revenue planning across product lines (moccasins, décolletés, sneakers/"all star")
- **Production Budget**: production planning, capacity, and cost allocation derived from the commercial forecast
- Supporting slide deck presenting the company background, product lines, and budget assumptions
